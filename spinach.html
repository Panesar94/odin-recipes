Creamed Spinach

Description

A steakhouse classic, luxurious creamed spinach with a hint of acid and a slight spice and nuttiness

Ingredients

  • 24 oz fresh baby spinach
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 red hot chilli pepper
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • Zest of 1 lemon juice of half
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • Fresh nutmeg

Directions

  1. Finely mince shallots, garlic and chillies
  2. Put butter in pan over medium heat until it foams
  3. Add in shallots arlic and chilli and sautee for about 2 mins till translucent
  4. Add spinach to the pan and cook untill it has wilted.
  5. Add cream cheese in and let it melt, then add in the heavy cream and lemon zest
  6. Reduce cream by half then add in parmesan and let it melt by stirring it in
  7. Grate fresh nutmeg and finish with fresh lemon juice

This creamed spinach is very well balanced and would go great alongside some steak!
